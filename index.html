The Barber

Meet Mark

Mark started barbering at the age of 18 and founded Wiseguys back in 2001 — making him Harpenden's most experienced and longest serving barber.

Located at the junction of Southdown Road and Piggottshill Lane in Southdown, Harpenden, Wiseguys offers a friendly, professional haircutting service with a personal touch.

Every appointment is booked online to ensure adequate time is allocated to each customer — no waiting around. Just quality cuts, great conversation, and a barber who genuinely cares about getting it right.
